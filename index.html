2023

This was an inverse facebook model where studios would spend money to acquire relevant cohorts of users and a large part of the value capture (about 70%) would be distributed back to players who completed tasks. Led investments, strategy and business for them and helped grow it from 0 to $2.5mn ARR.

2021
Quit ewards in 2021 beginning after I stumbled down the web3 rabbit hole. Started a gaming guild where we built a community of 50K active gamers in Phillipines and India and over ~$1mn in AUM. We helped over 10k gamers earn over $500k in 6 months.
